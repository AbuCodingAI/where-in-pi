🥧 Where in Pi?
A Professional Pattern Search Engine & Pi Discovery Tool
Have you ever wondered if your birthday, phone number, or even your name is hidden inside the infinite digits of Pi? Where in Pi is a powerful, high-speed tool designed to help you find any sequence—whether it's raw numbers or text—buried deep within the most famous number in mathematics.
🌟 What can you do?
1. Find Any Number Sequence
Search for any set of digits (like 12345 or your birthday 07121995) to see exactly where it first appears in the first million digits of Pi.
2. Search for Names and Words (ASCII)
Our system automatically converts text—like your name—into its unique computer "code" (ASCII) and then hunts for that pattern. For example, the name "AC" is mapped to the numbers 65 and 67, and we find exactly where 6567 is located.
3. Infinite Discovery
If your sequence isn't in our initial "Million-Digit" database, the system doesn't give up. It triggers a live calculation engine that generates more digits on-the-fly to keep the search going.
🚀 Getting Started
For Browsing (Web Interface)
Open the website in your favorite browser.
Select Your Type: Choose "Numbers" for raw digits or "Text" for names and words.
Enter Your Query: Type what you’re looking for in the search bar.
Hit Search: The system will instantly scan the data and provide the exact "Index" (the position) where your pattern begins.
🛠 How It Works (The Tech)
Built for Speed: Powered by Rust, one of the fastest programming languages in existence, ensuring your searches happen in milliseconds.
Smart Storage: We use a technique called "Memory Mapping" so the app stays fast and light, even when handling millions of digits.
Always Accurate: Our data is verified against professional mathematical standards to ensure every result is 100% correct.
